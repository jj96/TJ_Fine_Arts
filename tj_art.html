About the artist

Tabitha is a young, award-winning artist based in Edinburgh and Suffolk, where is studies and lives respectively. She specialises in landscapes and particularly enjoys working on canvasses, but she is also a keen photographer. Her online portfolio website here shows a selection of her works, which she hopes you will enjoy.
